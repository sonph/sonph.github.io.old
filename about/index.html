About Me

Me

My name is Son Pham, and welcome to my little corner on the Internet!

I'm a Computer Science undergraduate student at UT Dallas. My interests vary, including (but not limited to) the Linux operating system, open source software, web development and artificial intelligence.

Other than computers, I'm also into graphics and UI design, photography and guitars. Here's my first cover video, Sweet Child o Mine, from Spring 2013.
